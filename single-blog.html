An innovative concept

By Anvar | Jan 02, 2018

Wah Ustad! is the brainchild of Mr.Anvar and Mrs._____ who together have a combined 25 years of experience in the restaurant business and creative, distinctive cooking styles. This is their third venture which has become a rage among the discerning food lovers of Kochi.

Their style of cooking involves personal attention to every detail. Their specialty – the Biriyani, available in three variants of meats – Chicken, Mutton and Buffalo – are instant successes and accounts for the wild popularity of the restaurant among the young professionals of the city who take a ride from their offices to come all the way to Wah Ustad! only to savor the Biriyanis.

Though the cooking is overwhelmingly traditional in style, the owners have allowed themselves to be more creative with the Arabic and Mughlai dishes, tweaking them to be more loved by the Malayalee crowd with the infusion of much love spices and condiments.

The recent renovation and expansion of the restaurant have brought in more space and style to the restaurant. This has resulted in a more new and hip crowd thronging to the restaurant in recent times.
